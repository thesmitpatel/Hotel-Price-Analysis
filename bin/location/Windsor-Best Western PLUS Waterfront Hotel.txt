Perched along the banks of the Detroit River, the hotel offers views of the water and the downtown Detroit skyline from every room. A large, saltwater indoor pool, pet-friendly accommodations with mini-refrigerators, fitness center and business services create a welcoming retreat for executives and urban explorers alike. Custom touches from Keurig® coffee makers, to hypoallergenic feather pillows and spacious junior suites, complement the property’s central, city setting. Stay within steps of the Adventure Bay Family Aquatic Centre and St. Clair College Centre for the Arts, as well as within walking distance of the Windsor International Transit Centre, surrounding art galleries and the Windsor Caesar’s Casino. Check in on social media with complimentary high-speed internet from the comfort of your room, or explore nearby restaurants, from top-rated steakhouses to local delis and urban grills. With an attentive staff and professional audiovisual support, each space, from the lavish Waterfront and Great Lakes Ballrooms to the intimate Windsor Star Room lets you craft events that cater to your unique vision.
