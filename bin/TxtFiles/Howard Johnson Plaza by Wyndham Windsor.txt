Have fun at this Howard Johnson Plaza Hotel Windsor conveniently located off Highway 401 near downtown, shopping and Caesars Windsor Casino for gaming and live entertainment. Watch sporting events and concerts at Windsor Family Credit Union Centre, or attend an event at Joe Louis Arena and Cobo conference and exhibition center just 12 miles from this lodging. Find great breakfast and lunch onsite at the Marquis Restaurant, Start your day off with a free light breakfast item together with your choice of coffee, tea and juice. Host an event in the meeting rooms and banquet facilities, and stay productive in the business center. Complimentary large-vehicle parking makes it easy to bring a crowd. Connect to complimentary wireless Internet access from the comfort of your guest room, or upgrade to a hot tub room or suite for additional comfort. Smoke-free rooms are also available.
